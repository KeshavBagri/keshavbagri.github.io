About

Hi there! I'm Keshav. I'm an engineer with a master's degree in mechanical engineering from The Ohio State University (2024). From the very beginning, I was passionate about vehicles and robots which led me to pursue mechanical engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur (2022). Currently, I work as a Battery Algorithms Engineer at Lucid Motors where I develop algorithms for charging control, power estimation, and energy management as a part of their world-class battery management systems. Prior to this, I was a Graduate Research Associate under the guidance of Prof. Giorgio Rizzoni at the Center for Automotive Research working towards OSU's AutoDrive team participating in the General Motors - SAE AutoDrive Challenge II. I'm passionate about innovations in sustainability and automation focusing on vehicles and robots using the tools from control theory and optimization. Lastly, I'm always looking out for solutions to reduce human effort in regression by harnessing the power of computation.

I am a curious cat and my search history is the evidence for the same! I am also inclined towards blogging in my free time. Occasionally, I do spend time writing poems corresponding to various genres. In a nutshell, I'm a wholesome nerd: sci-fi movies/shows, passion for technology, geeky ideas, you name it. So if we ever meet in-person, bear in mind that I'm just a regular human being with slighlty different interests and most importantly with a deep and profound love for cars. ;)
